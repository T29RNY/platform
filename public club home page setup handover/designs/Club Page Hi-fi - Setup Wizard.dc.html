EVERY FIELD EXPLAINS ITSELF
No grassroots volunteer should wonder what a field does. So every step carries a plain "what this does" line, and the Sections step shows the live-data source under each block. Three rules:
1
Pre-filled. Every step starts from data the club already has.
2
Skippable. A sensible default means the page is never empty.
3
Discipline-aware. Picking football/boxing/gym pre-selects sections & wording.
Section keys match CLUB_PAGE_COMPOSITION_SPEC.md exactly — the wizard and the page renderer share one contract. The same steps reopen individually from the manage dashboard.
