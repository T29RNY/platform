Dashboard = wizard's building blocks, but non-linear & always-on. Every section deep-links straight to its editor. Draft ⇄ Published with discard is global.
Cross-cutting rules (apply to all three surfaces)
Live & actionable over static — scores, availability & next-fixture surface motion wherever data allows.
Per-club theming — 3 chosen colours + crest drive everything; system adapts to any palette, no hardcoded look.
Accessibility — legible at worst plausible colours; ≥44px tap targets; one-handed mobile.
Modular — every section independently on/off; page reads complete at 3 sections or all 8.
Out of scope: multi-photo galleries · video hosting · deep CMS page-builders · custom theming beyond 3-colour · e-commerce
